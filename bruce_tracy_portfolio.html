Who I Am

My name is Bruce Tracy, and I have lived in Halifax, Nova Scotia for most of my life. You may hear me brag about this beautiful spot from time to time, as it has been the inspiration for a lot of my artwork. I have been an artist for more than thirty years. Most of my work has been done in acrylic and oil paints and pencil. Over the past few years, I have branched out into the digital media. To me, the next logical step was to learn how to design and develop websites. So, here I am. Building my website and websites for my clients. Currently, I offer services in graphic (logo, business card and sign) design, animation, web design and development and online marketing. As my business grows, I will be offering more services. So, stay tuned!

Competencies

I have been working in graphic and web design for more than five years. I am now taking the next logical step in my professional development, which is web programming.

  • Adobe Photoshop
    Photoshop is one of my favourite tools to work with. You can alter pictures in any way you can imagine. You can also create new images to look like drawings, oil paintings, or water colour paintings.
  • Adobe Illustrator
    Adobe Illustrator is a powerful tool for creating vector images and you can even turn them into SVG's.
  • Adobe After Effects
    I use Adobe after Effects for almost all of my animation projects. I also use it as a film editing tool.
